maandag 23 mei 2016

It’s always hard to keep completely schtum whilst working on an exciting launch, especially when things like this take almost a year from start to shelves. I’m super excited to share with you the Sweet Inspirations range, comprising of 8 products smelling of sweet macarons and housed in beautiful pastel french patisserie style packaging. I am obsessed with this scent and whole range and from the 27th May you can order online at Feelunique and Superdrug and as of the 6th June, it will be in Superdrug stores across the UK *excited squeal*.
